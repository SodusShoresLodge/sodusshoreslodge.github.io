All Rooms

Here at the Sodus Shores Lodge, we have four different rooms available. Any room is perfect for a weekend getaway, bachelor/bachelorette parties, fishermen and ice fishermen, snowmobilers, charter captains, we have had them all.

All Room Amenities

Each room comes with the following amenities:

-Cable T.V.
-Mini Refrigerator
-Access to Bathrooms with Showers
-Air Conditioning
-Heat

All Rooms Have a Strict No-Smoking and No Pet Policy

We Also Ask that a Mask is Worn in Common Areas

Room 1:

Room 1 has one queen bed and all the things listed in the amenities. Pictured here to the right, it is perfect for one or two people staying however long you need. The bathroom is right down the hall and is located near the front door.


Room 2:

Room 2 has one queen bed and a single bed with all the listed amenities. Pictured to the right it can sleep one to three people comfortably. The bathroom is also right down the hall and is also located near the front door.


Room 3:

Room 3 along with the other amenities has a queen bed, a single bed, and a sink in the room. This is the only room with a sink and mirror inside making it easier to wash up quickly, brush your teeth, or do anything else you would need to all in your own room. This room is also conveniently located next to the bathroom and is down the hall a little ways from the front door.


Room 4:

Room 4 comes with all the other amenities, a queen bed, and a single bed. Located just down the hall from the bathroom and furthest from the front door. This room is furthest from the front door which provides it with a little bit more privacy than the other rooms.

Restrooms:

In the middle of all four rooms, there are men and women's bathrooms. They both include a shower and towels.
